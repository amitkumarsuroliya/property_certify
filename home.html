What are Energy Performance Certificates?

Energy Performance Certificates also known as EPCs show how energy efficient a property is. The certificate will provide a rating of the energy efficiency and CO2 emissions of a property from A grade the highest to grade G being very inefficient.

Who needs an EPC?

All buildings in the UK whether residential or commercial that are constructed, sold or rented out will need to have an Energy Performance Certificate.
